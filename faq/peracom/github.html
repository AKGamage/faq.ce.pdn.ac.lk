Steps to get onboard GitHub
  1. Go to https://github.com/
  2. Click the Sign up button at the top right corner
  3. Sign up using your eng email address and a password and username of your choice.
    4. Note: Keep the username short, simple, and all lowercase. Refer to this for some best practices.
  4. After creating your account, go to https://url.ce.pdn.ac.lk/e19-github/ and enter your email and GitHub username in the corresponding row.
  5. Wait until the cepdnaclk organization invitation is sent to your email by an admin.
  6. Accept the organization invitation.
  7. Verify that you have joined the organization by clicking Teams -> E19 -> Members and see whether your name appears there. Tick the corresponding cell in column E if your name appears in the E19 team.
